Visiting Planet Glorp

Everything you need to know

How to get to Planet Glorp

  1. Leave earth via rocket
  2. Take a left at Jupiter
  3. Continue straight for another 35 lightyears
  4. Arrive at your destination, Planet Glorp!

What to bring to Planet Glorp

  1. Human food and drinking water (the glorpians eat a linoleum-based diet)
  2. Your translator device* (duh, they don't speak English over there)
  3. Fuel for the journey home (Gas prices are ridiculous on Planet Glorp)

*translator device

Why you should visit Planet Glorp

  1. Its the closest inhabited planet to Earth!
  2. The Glorpians are very kind and welcoming.
  3. The food is amazing.
